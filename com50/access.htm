Through this website, users of our (secure) services will be able to:

  • Register their interest in our services.
  • Prepare and submit their works, including:
    • Information about a candidate release, including all the data necessary to identify works, genres, recording and copyright details, etc.
    • Artwork, and if desired - the tracks themselves (although for longer releases an uncompressed physical CD with the release in its ready form is preferred).
  • Track release progress
  • Track sales reports and payments

Following submission and approval, Republic Of Music packages the release and transmits the music, artwork, data and information to the online store, where it is added to their catalogue and made available for sale according to their internal processes.

In the meantime, you should contact us to register your interest in our services.
