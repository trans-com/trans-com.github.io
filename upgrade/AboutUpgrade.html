about UPGRADE
  
TRANSCOM, Brisbane's first established electronic music label, is proud to announce its involvement in the Next Level festival. 
Exclusively conceived and curated by TransCom for the Next Level festival, UPGRADE offers Brisbane's fans of electronic music - whatever age they may be - a chance to celebrate electronic music in all its forms and the use of technology in music production and performance. Held at the QUT Creative Industries Precinct, Kelvin Grove from 26-28 of October 2007, the Upgrade program will offer 8 live electronic music performances, DJs, a hybrid media installation, a music forum, and FREE music downloads from our Upgrade Download Station.
On Saturday 27th October, UPGRADE will feature 8 of Australia's finest electronic music bands; including six Brisbane acts with MY NINJA LOVER, PONYLOAF, VICTOR XRAY, TYCHO BRAHE, DIZZYGOTHECA and LOVELESS. Trans:Com is very excited to announce that SEVERED HEADS (Sydney) and BOXCAR (Sydney) will be returning to Brisbane to appear as headlining acts for the Upgrade event.
