About the "SILHOUETTE SERIES" 

Silhouette... influential music in profile... TRANS:COM's main aim behind the development of the SILHOUETTE SERIES is to give support to Brisbane’s independent electronic music makers. With Trans:Com as curator, the series provides an organised supportive framework for new and emerging electronic music performers, offering more ways for them to present and communicate with their live audience. As a quarterly event (March, May, July, & September), we can provide a consistent program whilst also tapping into an established network of supportive industry players in the areas of sound production (TONE PACER, a production & recording facility), music distribution/delivery/promotion (TRANS:COM), media (MAP MAGAZINE, TIME OFF & PULSERADIO.NET) and venue (BRISBANE POWERHOUSE). Through the Silhouette Series, TRANS:COM will also be experimenting with various technologies as part of its new  focus on purely digital distribution.  The Series is also an avenue for us to show our respect to some of our most influential electronic-influenced songwriters. For 2006, Trans:Com will be acknowledging the efforts of Gary Numan, Bernard Sumner, Martin Gore, and Bjork, and we hope to continue the program into 2007 with other key influences.  Each event will incorporate an audiovisual montage produced by an emerging local multimedia artist, interspersed with live performances from some of Brisbane's finest electronica bands and artists (many of which will no doubt include a tribute version or two).  If you would like to be kept up-to-date about the series and receive our FREE quarterly e-news updates about events like the Silhouette series (the artists, our label, and other interesting related news) just email anna@trans.com.au and request to become a Trans:Com subscriber.
