Information

Transmission Communications welcomes other independent electronica releases from Australian artists and labels for inclusion on this site's catalogue. Stock is handled on a consignment basis.

Submit the following details about each title you wish to have considered for the catalogue, and send a single sample to us. We will check the title/s out and get back to you regarding the details of interaction. We will also need a paragraph or two describing the release as you see fit, to appear on the catalogue.

Generally speaking, in terms of handling stock, the best method is to consign 3-5 copies of each title so we can fulfil orders quickly. Our address for samples and stock is: TransCom Electronica, PO Box 6291, Fairfield Q4103 Australia.
