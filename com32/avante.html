Lost Time Accident: Age 2 Wonder At Age 2 Wonder At is based on an 'ever-evolving' underscore derived from compositions created by Andrew Garton in 1982. These pieces were produced by relocating randomly- articulated tonal clusters in intervalic progressions - a technique originally devised by Australian composer Sykes Rose based on the plotting of arbitary chord structures across a simple grid. These compositions were reinvigorated when applied to Koan generative music software, creating 'ever-changing' renditions of each composition. they were then recomposed in real-time in collaboration with Simon Kaufmann. Age 2 Wonder At is thus an archive of a generative and interactive compositional process which evolved through improvisation, sampling and spatialisation techniques. Secession:
