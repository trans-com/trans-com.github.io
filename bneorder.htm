BNE celebrates 35 Years of Brisbane Independent Electronic Music.

Including a beautifully packaged coffee table style book and companion music archive (on a custom USB device) featuring 140 bands and over 260 tracks (nearly 24 hours of rare and exclusive music, much of it unavailable for many years), BNE provides electronic music lovers with a complete exploration of 35 years of Brisbane-based independent electronic music performance and production. The BNE book includes comprehensive band histories, photos, cover art, event flyers, and scene insights. It is the most comprehensive archive of a city's independent electronic music scene ever produced anywhere in the world.

Brisbane has always been a unique and sometimes challenging place for artistic endeavour, particularly with respect to our city's social, political and geographic context, and BNE captures the spirit of true innovation in the modern art form that is electronic music. There are some wonderful stories in BNE, but most importantly there is a lot of amazing music.
